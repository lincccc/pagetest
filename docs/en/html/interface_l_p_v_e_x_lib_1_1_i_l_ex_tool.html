Parameters
hdcHandle to the device context of the destination window or bitmap.
zoomXFactor for zooming in or out in the x-coordinate direction. By default, it's in the true scale.
zoomYFactor for zooming in or out in the y-coordinate direction. Setting to 0 means same zoom factor as zoomX, aka. isotropic scaling.
panXtranslation in the x-coordinate direction for panning. The final display x position is calculated as: \((x + panX) \times zoomX\)
panYtranslation in the y-coordinate direction for panning. The final display y position is calculated as: \((y + panY) \times zoomY\)
